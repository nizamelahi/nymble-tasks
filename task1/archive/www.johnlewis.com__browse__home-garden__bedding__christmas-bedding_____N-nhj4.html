Christmas Bedding

Christmas bedding is a great way to extend the festive trimmings across your home. We offer a variety of Christmas bedding styles that range from minimalist patterns such as star-patterned duvet covers and pillowcase sets, as well as more apparent prints. Explore our entire collection of Christmas bedding and Christmas cushions to find your perfect seasonal pieces.

  • What is the best Christmas bedding for children?

    Our own little home at John Lewis collection is designed especially for children and hence we have several fun Christmas bedding styles within that range, from cute snowmen to Christmas trees and reindeer.

  • What are good examples of Christmas bedding accessories?

    Alternatively to Christmas bedding, we also have a huge selection of Christmas cushions which can add a more subtle festive theme to your bedding as opposed to an entire duvet set. Opt for a Christmas cushion featuring a row of robins, Christmas trees, wintry pine forests or a family of polar bears, all in various shades to match accordingly to your choice of bedding. You can even pick Christmas cushions in the shape of stars alongside more conventional cushion configurations.
